# Atlanta Dog Training Landing Page — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Bold American Sports Brand
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** American Sports / Athletic Brand (Nike, Under Armour energy applied to local service)
**Core Principles:** High contrast, commanding typography, action-oriented, trust through authority
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0d2b5e) + electric yellow (#f5c518) + white. Navy conveys trust and professionalism; yellow signals urgency and value.
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with diagonal cuts between sections. Left-heavy asymmetric content blocks.
**Signature Elements:** Bold uppercase condensed headlines, yellow accent bars/underlines, paw print motif
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states that "charge up" — yellow glow on buttons, card lifts
**Animation:** Entrance animations on scroll (fade-up), button pulse on CTA
**Typography System:** Oswald (bold display) + Source Sans 3 (body)
</text>

## Approach 3: Direct-Response Conversion Machine ← SELECTED
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** High-conversion direct-response marketing (inspired by Syracuse Dog Training, Agora-style landing pages)
**Core Principles:** Outcome-focused headlines, social proof front and center, clear value proposition, urgency without gimmick
**Color Philosophy:** Royal blue (#1a5fa8) + signal yellow (#f5c518) + near-black (#1a1a1a). Blue = trust/authority, Yellow = value/action, Black = seriousness
**Layout Paradigm:** Sticky nav → full-bleed hero with booking card → value strip → features → videos → reviews → booking CTA → footer. Every section drives toward one action.
**Signature Elements:** Yellow CTA buttons everywhere, star ratings, "$47 value" badge, Google review cards with avatar initials
**Interaction Philosophy:** Every interactive element has a clear affordance. Hover = slight lift + brightness. CTAs pulse subtly.
**Animation:** Scroll-triggered fade-up on review cards and feature cards. Hero text slides in from left.
**Typography System:** Source Sans 3 900 (headlines) + Source Sans 3 400/600 (body). Uppercase tracking on eyebrows.
</text>
<probability>0.09</probability>
</response>

## Selected: Approach 3 — Direct-Response Conversion Machine

Rationale: This is a booking landing page. The goal is one conversion: get the visitor to book and pay $47. Every design decision must serve that goal. The Syracuse site uses this exact philosophy — bold blue/yellow, big headline, booking card in the hero, social proof below. We'll match and elevate it.
